## Inventory Management
### Uploading Inventory via Excel/CSV
1. Go to the Inventory Management page.
2. Click 'Upload Inventory'.
3. Click 'Select Excel File' and choose a `.xlsx`, `.xls`, or `.csv` file.
4. (Optional) Download the template for correct formatting.
5. Review the preview table for errors or warnings.
6. Click 'Upload' to confirm.

![Inventory Upload Modal](screenshots/inventory-upload.png)

**Template Format:**
- Required: Item Name, Department, Quantity
- Optional: Category, Location, Reorder Point, Safety Stock, Unit Cost

### Manual Inventory Entry
1. Click 'Add Item'.
2. Fill in the item details.
3. Click 'Save'.

### Editing & Deleting Items
- Use the action buttons in the inventory table to edit or delete items.

![Edit Inventory Item](screenshots/edit-item.png)
